Introduction

The HS Division and Percentage Calculator is an online calculation tool. It helps the students to calculate the percentage, grade and division by using their total marks. And students can also calculate using their marks of individual subjects.

This tool is especially useful for students who want to know their percentage, grade and division for higher institutional admission.

Formula of Uchcha Madhyamik Percentage Calculator

Percentage = (Total Marks Obtained / Total Maximum Marks) * 100

Here, Total Marks Obtained = First Language + Second Language + Subject 3 + Subject 4 + Subject 5 (best 3 subjects + first and second language is compulsory)
Total Maximum Marks = 500

To calculate the percentage of total marks obtained in Uchcha Madhyamik, we first divide the total obtained marks by maximum marks. Then we multiply it into 100.

Formula of Uchcha Madhyamik Grade Calculator

Grades are usually assigned based on percentage ranges. For Uchcha Madhyamik:

  • AA (90% to 100%): Outstanding
  • A+ (80% to 89%): Excellent
  • A (70% to 79%): Very good
  • B+ (60% to 69%): Good
  • B (50% to 59%): Satisfactory
  • C (30% to 49%): Marginal
  • D (below 30%): Disqualified

To calculate the grade, we first calculate the percentage. Then we check according to this chart.

Formula of Uchcha Madhyamik Division Calculator

Divisions are based on total marks obtained. For Uchcha Madhyamik:

  • Superstar First Division: total obtained marks >= 450
  • Star First Division: total obtained marks >= 400
  • High First Division: total obtained marks >= 350
  • First Division: total obtained marks >= 300
  • Second Division: total obtained marks >= 250
  • Third Division: total obtained marks >= 150
  • No Division: total obtained marks < 150

To calculate the division, we first calculate the total obtained marks. Then we check according to this chart.

Formula of HS Pass or Fail Calculator

In Uchcha Madhyamik, students need to achieve a minimum of 30 marks in every subject to pass. Uchcha Madhyamik pass mark is 150 out of 500, i.e. 30%. Here, to pass or fail calculations, students need to fill in all the subject boxes.

Example of HS Division and Percentage Calculator

Suppose 460 is the total obtained marks of a student. In this case, the output is:

  • Total Score: 460
  • Percentage: 65.71%
  • Grade: A (Very good)
  • Division: First Division
  • Status: Pass

How to calculate HS division?

  • Superstar First Division: This is the highest division, awarded when a student scores 450 marks or more out of a maximum of 500 marks.
  • Star First Division: Students who score between 400 and 449 marks fall into this division.
  • High First Division: Students who score between 350 and 399 marks fall into this division.
  • First Division: This division is for students scoring between 300 and 349 marks.
  • Second Division: Students scoring between 250 and 299 marks fall into this division.
  • Third Division: Students scoring between 150 and 249 marks fall into this division.
  • No Division: Students scoring below 150 marks do not receive a division.
